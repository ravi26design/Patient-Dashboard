Daily Check-In
7 quick steps · about 60 seconds · +20 XP
Urge surfing
An urge is a wave.
It rises, crests, and falls — every time. You don't have to act on it. You just have to ride it until it passes.
1
Notice where you feel the craving in your body. Name it: "tightness in my chest," "buzzing in my hands."
2
Breathe into that spot. Don't push the urge away — just watch it like a wave moving through you.
3
Rate the wave 0–10. Keep breathing. Watch the number rise… and then start to drop.
4
It always comes down. You surfed it. You didn't have to use to get to the other side.
5-4-3-2-1 grounding
Use your senses to land here.
5
things you can see
Look around. Name them out loud.
4
things you can touch
Feel the texture of each one.
3
things you can hear
Near sounds, far sounds.
2
things you can smell
Or two scents you like.
1
thing you can taste
Water, gum, a deep breath.
4-7-8 breathing
BREATHE IN
Inhale through your nose · 4
Breathe in for 4, hold for 7, exhale slowly for 8. Follow the circle. Four rounds is usually enough to feel your body settle.
